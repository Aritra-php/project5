About Us

We are a growing startup company whose main office is located in Durgapur WestBengal. People nowerdays if they want to do proper laundry they have to carry their pack of cloths to laundry shop which is either close to them or far away from them. Now it is easy for some people but there are many who are busy throughout the day. This websote is for them.We at laundry.com first ask our user to enter their address and city in the registration form then our system finds out a laundry shop nearby to their place, the shop will take the order.Someone from the laundry shop will come and take the cloths, do the laundry and then return it back on the estimated day of delievery. This is what we do make life easier for people.
